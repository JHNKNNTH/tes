About Us

Crest Harvest Enterprises

Crest Harvest Enterprises is a palay and Rice Trading started in 2012. We buy newly harvested palay in the farm. We have agents in different areas(Luzon) to give us information about variety, price, and quality of palay in their area. Testing and classifying are to be made by our Classifier using test kit/palay tester prior giving our price to the farmer. Once the farmer agrees on our price, palay are to be weight. Once done, payment to be followed, Newly harvested palay to be delivered in our drying facility for drying. Dried palay are then delivered to rice mill in Nueva Ecija and Bulacan.

Nowadays, harvesting palay becomes faster than before. Combine harvester can harvest more than a double compare to the old threshing system. Drying methods must be develop to facilitate the drying demand due to fast harvesting/post harvest mechanization. "Mechanical Grain Dryer" is the modern technology that can help facilitate the drying demand. This equipment can save grain in cloudy or rainy day, and prevent them from mold and sprout. Moldy grains produce aflatoxin which could cause cancer. Aflatoxin could even survive under 280 degrees celcius. Human could also be infected after consuming poultry which ate the aflatoxin contaminated grain. Mechanical grain dryer also saves space for drying. It only needs an area of 6.50sq.m., for 12 tons of palay. Mechanical Grain Dryer can run 24 hours fully automatic, whenever day or night, cloudy or rainy day. One person can manage thousand tons of grains dried in a day. It also ensures even moisture content, and avoid losses due to over drying, and reduce broken rice. We, at Crest Harvest Enterprises are innovating to get along with the mechanization. We have existing solar dryer and Mechanical grain dryer as well. However, the said drying equipment is still insufficient with regards to the bulk harvested palay during harvest season.
